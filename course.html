Oral Suturing Practical Course

Register Now

What You Will Learn

This hands-on course teaches the essential principles and techniques of oral suturing used in dental and surgical practice.

  • Wound healing and tissue management
  • Types of sutures and suture materials
  • Needle and instrument selection
  • Proper instrument handling
  • Basic knot-tying methods
  • Simple interrupted sutures
  • Continuous sutures
  • Horizontal and vertical mattress sutures
  • Flap closure and tension-free suturing
  • Common mistakes and how to avoid them
  • Practical exercises on suturing models

Course Outcomes

By the end of this course, participants will be able to handle suturing instruments safely, select suitable suture materials, and perform basic oral suturing techniques with confidence.

Target Audience

  • Dentistry Students
  • Newly Graduated Dentists
  • Healthcare Students
  • Professionals Seeking to Improve Suturing Skills

Visual Dental Check Course

Register Now

What You Will Learn

The Visual Dental Check Course trains participants to perform systematic visual oral examinations, identify common oral health problems, and support early detection.

  • Introduction to Visual Dental Check
  • Ethical behavior during examination
  • Patient records and diagnosis forms
  • Extraoral examination techniques
  • Normal and abnormal findings
  • Dental caries detection
  • Tooth sensitivity assessment
  • Tooth wear and cracked teeth
  • Tooth misalignment
  • Basic periodontal examination
  • Oral soft tissue examination
  • Documentation and reporting

Course Outcomes

By the end of this course, participants will be able to perform a visual dental check, recognize common oral diseases, document findings, and support oral health promotion.

Course Modules

  • Module I: Foundations of Visual Dental Check
  • Module II: Extraoral Examination
  • Module III: Intraoral Hard Tissue Examination
  • Module IV: Intraoral Soft Tissue Examination

Target Audience

  • Dentistry Students
  • Dental Professionals
  • Healthcare Students
  • Oral Health Volunteers
