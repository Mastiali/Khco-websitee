Oral Suturing Practical Course

Register Now

Description

What You Will Learn

This hands-on course is designed to teach participants the essential principles and techniques of oral suturing used in dental and surgical practice. Through practical training and expert guidance, participants will develop confidence in handling suturing instruments and performing common suturing procedures.

  • Introduction to wound healing and tissue management
  • Types of sutures and suture materials
  • Selection of appropriate needles and instruments
  • Proper instrument handling techniques
  • Basic knot-tying methods
  • Simple interrupted sutures
  • Continuous sutures
  • Horizontal and vertical mattress sutures
  • Principles of flap closure and tension-free suturing
  • Common suturing mistakes and how to avoid them
  • Infection control and patient safety during suturing procedures
  • Practical exercises on suturing models

Course Outcomes

By the end of this course, participants will be able to safely handle suturing instruments, select suitable suture materials, perform basic oral suturing techniques, apply proper wound closure principles, and improve their clinical skills for future dental and surgical procedures.

Target Audience

  • Dentistry Students
  • Newly Graduated Dentists
  • Healthcare Students Interested in Surgical Skills
  • Professionals Seeking to Improve Their Suturing Techniques
