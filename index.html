Supporting Health, Membership, and Community Care

The Kurdistan Health Care Organization website is a central digital platform designed to support members, share healthcare-related services, and improve communication between the organization and the community.

Member Portal

About KHCO

KHCO works to strengthen community health support through organized membership services, awareness activities, educational programs, and healthcare partnerships.

Our Main Objective

The main objective of the KHCO website is to provide a central digital platform that connects members, delivers healthcare-related services, and improves communication between the organization and the community.

Through this platform, KHCO can present its mission, publish activities, highlight services, and provide clear information about member benefits such as digital identification cards, medical insurance support, and course discounts.

The website also helps the organization maintain transparency, build trust, and create stronger engagement with the public.

Our Services

KHCO provides practical services that support its members and strengthen health-related community engagement.

Medical Insurance Support

KHCO provides medical insurance support for members through collaboration with Anwarsheikha Medical City.

Course Discounts

Members receive a 38% discount on KHCO courses, and certificates are provided free of charge.

Digital Identification

Most members have received their digital identification cards, with hard copy printing planned after final revision.

Laboratory Discount

Members receive a 20% discount in Anwarsheikha Lab as part of KHCO medical support benefits.

Pharmacy Discount

Members receive a 7% discount in Anwarsheikha Pharmacy in Anwarsheikha Medical City.
